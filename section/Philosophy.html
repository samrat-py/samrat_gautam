Philosophy

Small Steps at a Time

Growth doesn’t have to be fast — it just has to be consistent. I believe in learning one thing at a time, building project by project, and trusting that steady effort leads to big results.

Progress Over Perfection

I focus on moving forward rather than waiting for things to be perfect. Every imperfect project is a stepping stone to better skills, clearer thinking, and real-world experience.

Stay Curious, Stay Growing

Curiosity is what keeps me going. Whether it’s trying out a new framework or solving a tricky bug, I treat every challenge as a chance to learn something new and expand what I can do.
